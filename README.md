# semantic-field
This repository contains high-level notes, essays, and conceptual views that emerged in the course of reflection and work.
They are not specifications, not recommendations, and not claims of completeness.
They exist as fixed traces of thought - open to reading, interpretation, or simple passing by.

These notes and essays are part of a broader inquiry into AI ethics - not only as a normative field, but as an algorithmic and procedural phenomenon.
Many of the formulations emerged in dialogue with AI, used here as a reflective instrument rather than an autonomous author.
